📋 How to pick your place Pro tips
📍

Location first

Prioritize proximity to campus, public transport, and grocery stores. Walkability matters.

💰

Budget wisely

Factor in rent + utilities + deposit. Look for all-inclusive deals to avoid surprises.

🔒

Safety & security

Check for secure entry, CCTV, good lighting, and emergency contact availability.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Community vibe

Visit common areas, talk to current tenants, and ask about noise levels and social life.

📄

Read the fine print

Understand lease terms, cancellation policies, and what's included (WiFi, laundry, etc.).

🛠️

Inspect thoroughly

Check plumbing, appliances, internet speed, and pest control before signing anything.
